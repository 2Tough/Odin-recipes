Buniuelos

Description

You can see this a very tasty dish. Made with yuca and cheese. It is accompanied my honey and is very tasty.

You don't require more than yuca and cheese to make this dish. Most of the time to make it goes to the grating the main two ingredients.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound of yuca
  • 1 pound of cheese
  • 400 ml of honey
  • 300 ml of oil
Steps
  1. Grate the yuca and cheese into a bowl
  2. Mix both and make small units from them
  3. Put the small balls into the hot oil and fry them
  4. Once finished, serve with honey on the side

Gallo pinto

Description

Easy to cook and tasty for eating. This dish is popular because it's easy to cook, ingredient are available and very tasty

There are multiple variations and every family might add their own touch. Some of the most polular variations include the spicy rice and beans, which is served with spicy chicken.

Ingredients
  • 500g of cooked rice
  • 500g of cooked beans
  • 50ml of oil
Steps
  1. Mix the rice and beans into a bowl with hot oil
  2. Cook for 5 minutes
  3. Optional: Add spices and salt to your liking
